About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Bertrand. I am a hard working event management professional with 4+ years of experience in the Information Security Industry. I currently work as an Event Manager for IANS, overseeing all of our 2-day conferences and smaller 5 hour deep-dive Symposiums. I started working with basic html when I was tasked to edit our registration pages. I really enjoyed learning HTML which got me exploring options to learn further about coding and development.

I am currently enrolled in the UNH Coding Boot Camp to expand my career skills to hopefully make a career change into some coding or development job. In my free time I really enjoy skiing, golfing and running.
